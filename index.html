Xiaolong Ma

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate and research assistant in Materials Science at NC State University, advised by Prof. Yuntian Zhu. Meanwhile, I'm also pursuing a master's degree in Statistics. My research interests focus on microstructure-property relationship in nanomaterials and laminate/gradient structures. Electron microscopy and statistical methods are great tools to characterize materials' microstructure and properties in my studies. Interdisciplinary knowledges always refresh my perspective and hold the potential to trigger new ideas.

Before joining NC State, I was an undergrad at Zhejiang University, China, where I obtained a Bachelor of Science in Materials Science with Chu Kochen Honors Degree.
